How to Use This Tool

  1. Upload your photo - Click the "Upload Your Photo" button and select an image from your device. A preview will be shown.
  2. Choose background color - Select the desired background color for your passport photo.
  3. Select photo size - Choose from standard photo sizes for different document types.
  4. Set number of copies - Select how many copies you want on one sheet.
  5. Generate photos - Click the "Generate Passport Photos" button.
  6. Save your photo - Right-click on the result canvas and select "Save Image As...".

Tips for Best Results

  • Use a clear, high-quality photo with good, even lighting on your face.
  • Ensure your face is clearly visible, centered, and looking directly at the camera.
  • A plain, contrasting background in your original photo helps with automatic background removal.
  • Follow official guidelines for passport/visa photos in your country regarding expression, head covering, glasses, etc.

About Passport Photo Maker

This free online tool helps you create passport-style photos. Our AI-powered technology attempts to automatically remove the background and formats your photo to the selected size and copy count.

Features

  • Attempted automatic background removal
  • Multiple photo size options
  • Custom background colors
  • Multiple copies on one sheet
  • Client-side processing: Your images are processed in your browser and not uploaded to a server.
  • Free to use
