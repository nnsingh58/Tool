Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What types of data can I encode in a QR code?

A: Our QR Code Generator supports URLs, plain text, email addresses, phone numbers, Wi-Fi credentials, and more. You can encode any text-based information.

Q: Are there any size limits for QR code data?

A: QR codes have a maximum capacity depending on the data type and error correction level. For URLs, we recommend keeping them under 2000 characters for best results.

Q: Can I customize the appearance of the QR code?

A: Currently, our generator creates standard black and white QR codes for maximum compatibility. We're working on adding customization options in the future.

Q: Are the generated QR codes free to use commercially?

A: Yes, all QR codes generated by our tool are completely free to use for both personal and commercial purposes without any restrictions.

Q: Can I scan these QR codes with any smartphone?

A: Absolutely! Our QR codes are standard and compatible with all QR code readers on smartphones, including built-in camera apps on iOS and Android.
