Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What document formats can I convert?

A: Our Document Format Converter supports PDF, DOCX, TXT, HTML, and JSON formats. You can convert between any of these formats.

Q: Is there a file size limit for conversion?

A: For optimal performance, we recommend files under 10MB. Larger files may take longer to process.

Q: Can I convert multiple files at once?

A: Currently, our converter processes one file at a time. You can convert multiple files sequentially.

Q: Will my document formatting be preserved?

A: We strive to maintain formatting as much as possible, but some complex formatting may not transfer perfectly between formats.

Q: Is the conversion process secure?

A: Yes, your files are processed securely and are not stored on our servers after conversion is complete.
