Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What calculations can I perform?

A: Our Multifunction Calculator supports Basic arithmetic (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division), Currency conversion, Area conversion, Weight conversion, Distance conversion, and Percentage calculations.

Q: Are the currency conversion rates accurate?

A: We use fixed conversion rates for major currencies (USD, INR, EUR). For real-time rates, we recommend checking current market values as they fluctuate daily.

Q: Can I convert between any units?

A: Yes, our calculator supports conversion between various units for area, weight, distance, and more. We include both metric and imperial units, plus some traditional Indian units like Bigha.

Q: Is there a limit to the numbers I can calculate?

A: There's no strict limit, but extremely large numbers may cause precision issues due to JavaScript's floating-point arithmetic. For most everyday calculations, the tool works perfectly.

Q: Can I use this calculator offline?

A: Yes! Once loaded, the calculator works completely offline as all calculations are done locally in your browser.
