Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What video formats are supported?

A: Our Video Converter supports MP4, AVI, MKV, MOV, WMV, FLV, and many more formats. You can convert between virtually any video format.

Q: Is there a file size limit?

A: For optimal performance, we recommend videos under 500MB. Larger files may take longer to process and require more memory.

Q: How long does conversion take?

A: Conversion time depends on the file size, format, and your device's processing power. Most videos convert within a few minutes.

Q: Will the video quality be preserved?

A: We strive to maintain high quality during conversion. However, some quality loss may occur depending on the output format and compression settings.

Q: Can I convert videos on mobile devices?

A: Yes! The Video Converter is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. However, processing large videos on mobile may be slower due to hardware limitations.
