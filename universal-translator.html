Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How many languages are supported?

A: Our Universal Translator supports 50+ languages including Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Gujarati, Punjabi, and all major world languages like English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, and more.

Q: Is the translation accurate?

A: We use advanced machine translation algorithms that provide good accuracy for most common phrases and sentences. However, for critical or professional translations, we recommend human verification.

Q: What is the maximum text length I can translate?

A: You can translate up to 5000 characters at once. For longer texts, we recommend breaking them into smaller chunks for better accuracy and performance.

Q: Can I translate Hindi to English and vice versa?

A: Yes! Our tool provides full bidirectional translation between Hindi and all supported languages, including English, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, and many others.

Q: Is my translation data secure?

A: Yes, we take privacy seriously. Your translations are processed securely and are not stored on our servers after the session ends.
