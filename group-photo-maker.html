Group Photo Maker Tool

ToolHub's Group Photo Maker is a versatile online tool that allows you to create beautiful photo collages and grid layouts from multiple images. Whether you're organizing team photos, event memories, or social media content, this tool provides flexible customization options to create stunning group compositions. Perfect for businesses, schools, families, and content creators.

Our advanced grid algorithm automatically arranges your photos into an aesthetically pleasing layout with optional center image placement. You can customize frame dimensions, spacing between photos, and choose whether to include a scenic center image. The tool works entirely in your browser, ensuring your photos remain private and secure.

How to Use

  1. Upload multiple images using the file input (you can select several at once).
  2. Set your desired frame width and height in pixels (default 200x200).
  3. Adjust the gap between frames (default 10px).
  4. Choose whether to add a center scenic image or leave it blank.
  5. Click "Create Group Photo" to generate your collage.
  6. Download the resulting image from the canvas output.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What image formats are supported?

A: Our Group Photo Maker supports all common image formats including JPEG, PNG, GIF, and WebP. You can upload multiple images at once.

Q: Is there a limit to how many photos I can upload?

A: You can upload as many photos as you want. The tool will automatically arrange them into an optimal grid layout. For best results, we recommend 3-20 photos.

Q: Can I customize the frame size and spacing?

A: Yes! You can adjust the frame width and height in pixels, as well as the gap between frames. The default settings are 200x200px frames with 10px gaps.

Q: What is the center scenic image feature?

A: The center scenic image option adds a decorative image in the center of your collage for visual interest. You can choose to include it or leave the center blank.

Q: Can I use this tool on mobile devices?

A: Absolutely! The Group Photo Maker is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.
