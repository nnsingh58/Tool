Text to Speech

How to Use:

  • Enter the text you want to convert to speech in the box above.
  • Choose a voice from the dropdown menu.
  • Adjust the pitch using the slider (0 = low, 2 = high).
  • Click "Speak" to hear the text.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Text to Speech?

A: Text to Speech (TTS) is a technology that converts written text into spoken words. Our tool uses your browser's built-in speech synthesis capabilities to read text aloud.

Q: What voices are available?

A: The available voices depend on your operating system and browser. Common options include various accents and languages like English (US, UK, Australian), Hindi, Spanish, French, and more. The dropdown shows all voices installed on your system.

Q: Can I adjust the speech speed?

A: Yes, you can adjust the pitch using the slider (0 = low pitch, 2 = high pitch). The speech rate is set to normal (1x) for optimal clarity.

Q: Does Text to Speech work offline?

A: Yes! Our Text to Speech tool works completely offline once loaded. It uses the browser's built-in speech synthesis engine, which doesn't require an internet connection.

Q: Can I use this tool on mobile devices?

A: Absolutely! The Text to Speech tool is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. However, voice availability may vary depending on your device's operating system.
